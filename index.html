About Radha

Hello! I'm Radha, a passionate mehndi and nail artist with over 2 years of experience in creating stunning designs that capture the essence of tradition with a modern twist.

My journey began as a small hobby and has evolved into a successful business serving clients for weddings, special occasions, and everyday beauty enhancements. I believe that every design tells a story and reflects the personality of the wearer.

I use only premium, natural ingredients for mehndi and high-quality, long-lasting products for nail art. My priority is not just creating beautiful designs but ensuring a comfortable, enjoyable experience for every client.

Whether you're looking for an intricate bridal mehndi design or a subtle, elegant nail art pattern, I'm dedicated to bringing your vision to life with precision and care.
